2. Logs in with code → invites team members (dropdown of registered names)
3. Invited member logs in → accepts/rejects invite
4. Captain confirms "team is complete" (min 1, max 5 members)
5. Problem statements become visible ONLY after team confirmed
6. Quiz shown as optional helper alongside PS selection
7. Anyone in team can pick PS (not just captain)
