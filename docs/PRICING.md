**Effective:** [TBD: pricing live date — align with end of beta or 1 April 2027, whichever is earlier]

All prices quoted **+18% GST**. Always. Never quote inclusive — it confuses chapters used to seeing GST as a separate line and hides the real cost from their auditor.

## 1. The Four Tiers

### Free — "Programs Only"

- **Price:** ₹0
- **Includes:** Read access to YIP-2026 and YiFuture-2026 program data. Chapter chairs can view their chapter's participation, track nominations, see outcomes.
- **Plus:** Basic member list — read-only, **up to 50 members**.
- **Purpose:** This is the wedge. National program chairs need this. Once they're in, the Starter wall hits fast.

### Starter — ₹20,000/year + 18% GST

- **Total payable:** ₹23,600/year
- **Includes:** Up to 150 members (full CRUD), events module, basic finance (budget + expense entry, no recurring workflows or sponsorship pipeline).
- **The wall:** Hits when chapter crosses 150 members OR needs recurring expense workflows, approvals, sponsorship pipelines, or multi-vertical finance.

### Pro — ₹60,000/year + 18% GST

- **Total payable:** ₹70,800/year
- **Includes:** Unlimited members, full finance command center (sponsorships, approvals, reimbursements), stakeholder CRM, knowledge management, Take Pride awards automation, analytics, WhatsApp integration.
- **The wall:** Chapters running serious cross-stakeholder programs — schools, colleges, industries, NGOs, government — and that need to defend their finance to auditors and their performance to Yi National.

### Enterprise — Custom

- **Price:** Quoted per deal. Floor: **[TBD: ₹15L/year for Yi National multi-chapter deal — confirm after first national conversation]**.
- **Trigger:** When Director comes out of stealth and pitches to Yi National HQ for an all-India rollout. Not before.

## 2. Wall Design Rationale

**Why 150-member ceiling on Starter?**
Median Indian Yi chapter is 80-200 members (judgment from Erode + adjacent chapter knowledge — not confirmed across all chapters [TBD: validate with Yi National roster]). 150 puts the wall above the median but below the active top-third. Chapters that grow past 150 are exactly the ones with budget to upgrade.

**Why CRM is Pro tier, not Starter?**
CRM (schools, colleges, industries, government, NGOs, vendors) is the actual differentiator. A chapter that needs CRM is running real partnerships and has real money flowing. Putting it behind the Pro wall sorts the buyers from the browsers.

**Why analytics is Pro?**
Analytics matters to vertical heads and chair successions — both are Pro-tier user profiles. Starter chapters don't have the data volume for analytics to be useful. Giving it away in Starter would dilute the upgrade signal.

## 3. GST Handling

- Quote everything as base + 18% GST, separately. ₹20,000 + 18% GST = ₹23,600 total.
- Sole prop (Jicate Solutions) crosses the ₹20L GST registration threshold at roughly **33 Pro chapters or 100 Starter chapters**. Register for GST **before** that, not after — late registration triggers retroactive liability. Target: register at ₹15L cumulative billed.
- Issue tax invoices with GSTIN, HSN code (SAC 998314 — IT software services), and PAN.

---

## 4. Founding Chapter Discount

The first **10 paying chapters** lock in **50% off for 3 years**:
- Starter: ₹10,000/year + GST = ₹11,800
- Pro: ₹30,000/year + GST = ₹35,400

After 3 years, they renew at full rate (or the then-current rate). Make this explicit in the invoice line item: "Founding Chapter Discount — valid through [date]".

Rationale: First 10 are the riskiest customers we'll ever have. Lock them in. The discount is cheaper than losing them.

---

## 5. Billing Cadence

- **Annual billing is default.** One invoice, one payment, one year of access.
- **Monthly billing is an exception** — available only on request, and only for Pro tier. Add a **15% surcharge** for monthly (covers admin overhead and churn risk). Pro monthly = ₹5,750/month + GST.

---

## 6. TDS Handling

Chapters paying invoices to Jicate Solutions (sole prop) will deduct **10% TDS under Section 194J** (professional/technical services).

- Invoice ₹70,800 → chapter pays ₹63,720, deposits ₹7,080 as TDS, gives Omm a Form 16A.
- Omm claims the TDS credit at year-end ITR. It's refundable, not lost.
- Make this explicit in the contract: "Payment net of applicable TDS. TDS certificate (Form 16A) to be issued within 15 days of payment."

---

## 7. Refund Policy

**30-day money-back on annual plans.** No questions, no clawbacks on data. Refund processed within 14 days of request. Mid-year cancellations after day 30 — no refund, but account stays active until the paid-through date.

## 8. Multi-Chapter Regional Discount

**20% off** when 5+ chapters in the same Yi region buy together (single PO, single invoice):
- 5 Pro chapters in Tamil Nadu region = ₹60,000 × 5 × 0.80 = ₹2,40,000 + GST
- Discount applies on top of founding chapter discount only if the regional group buys within the founding 10 window. After that, regional discount is mutually exclusive with other discounts.

This unlocks regional convenor / Yi state-chair conversations as a sales channel.

## 9. Pvt Ltd Incorporation Trigger

Convert from sole proprietorship to Private Limited Company within **30 days** if ANY of these fire:

1. **First paying chapter signed** (any tier, any amount).
2. **₹2,00,000 cumulative revenue** received (sole prop liability is unlimited — capping exposure matters once real money flows).
3. **DPDP Act notice received** — any inquiry from data protection authorities. Sole prop has no liability shield for data breaches; Pvt Ltd has limited liability.

Incorporation budget: ~₹15,000 (registration + DSC + DIN + first-year compliance). Timeline: 10-15 working days via MCA portal. Continue using `director@jkkn.ac.in` as Director email; transfer Yi Connect IP and contracts from sole prop to Pvt Ltd via written assignment.
